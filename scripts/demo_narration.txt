Welcome to Roubaix—a build-ready experiment in graph-aware enterprise retrieval.

Here are the problems we are attacking. First, runaway cost: many AI stacks default to the most expensive retrieval path on every question. Second, relationship-heavy questions get shallow keyword answers when they need graph depth. Third, time-sensitive answers go stale without explicit freshness handling. And fourth, leadership lacks an audit trail that ties spend to routing decisions.

The business outcomes we are driving toward are measurable: lower cost per successful answer, lower latency by using the right path first, higher quality on multi-hop and relationship questions, and higher trust when answers must be current.

On this page you will see a live call into our API. The response includes which retrieval mode was selected—transparency you can connect to cost and quality reporting.

This scaffold uses deterministic routing today, with DSPy and GEPA optimization and AdalFlow-style runtime control on the roadmap. Thank you for watching.
